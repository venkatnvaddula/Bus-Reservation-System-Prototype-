rBus provides bus travellers, the most uncomplicated and hassle-free booking experience ever. Choose your destination, select bus based on user ratings, view the seat layout, choose convenient seats, and book your ticket in just a few clicks! The rBus experience does not end with the booking. In a series of industry leading features, rBus offers navigation to bus boarding points, real-time tracking of buses and information on rest stops.

rBus also partners with leading bus operators to provide exclusive ‘Red Deals” to customers booking on rBus.

A dedicated 24*7 customer-care team is available to cater to all the passengers needs while they are on-the- go.
